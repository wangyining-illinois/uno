This method tests whether the card that the current player intends to play out is valid, according to the last card that was played out. However, whether a player will be penalized will be checked later by \mbox{\hyperlink{classuno_1_1_server_a7bcc89ad1ad90798fe26614643877e3d}{should\+Be\+Penalized()}}. Therefore, one player may be penalized even though \mbox{\hyperlink{classuno_1_1_server_adeabcb25283e66b9be63913d8944f2fa}{valid\+Card()}} returns true.
